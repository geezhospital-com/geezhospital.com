Our Facilities

Geez Hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art medical technology and modern facilities to provide you with the best possible care.

Operating Theatres

Our advanced operating theatres are equipped with the latest surgical technology for a wide range of procedures, ensuring patient safety and optimal outcomes.

MRI & CT Scan

High-resolution MRI and CT scan machines for accurate and detailed diagnostic imaging, crucial for precise medical assessments.

Modern Laboratory

A fully equipped laboratory offering a comprehensive range of diagnostic tests, from routine blood work to specialized pathology, with rapid and reliable results.

Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

Our ICU provides critical care services for severely ill patients, staffed by highly skilled professionals and supported by advanced monitoring systems.

Emergency Department

24/7 emergency services with a dedicated team prepared to handle all types of medical emergencies efficiently and effectively.

Hospital Pharmacy

A well-stocked pharmacy providing a wide range of medications, managed by qualified pharmacists to ensure safe and appropriate drug dispensation.

Comfortable Patient Rooms

Spacious and comfortable patient rooms designed to provide a restful and healing environment, equipped with modern amenities for patient well-being.

Ultrasound and X-ray

Advanced ultrasound and X-ray facilities for non-invasive diagnostic imaging, essential for various medical assessments.
