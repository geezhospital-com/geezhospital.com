Our Services

Geez Hospital offers diverse and modern services provided by highly qualified specialists and sub-specialists.

Internal Medicine

Comprehensive diagnosis and treatment for adult diseases, focusing on non-surgical approaches and long-term health management.

Cardiology

Specialized care for heart and vascular conditions, including diagnostics, treatment, and preventive programs.

General Surgery

Offering a wide range of surgical procedures, from routine to complex operations, performed by skilled surgeons.

Pediatrics & Adolescents

Dedicated medical care for infants, children, and teenagers, covering their growth, development, and specific health issues.

Obstetrics & Gynecology

Comprehensive health services for women, including prenatal care, childbirth, postnatal care, and reproductive health.

ENT

Diagnosis and treatment of disorders related to the ear, nose, and throat, including head and neck conditions.

Radiology

Advanced diagnostic imaging services such as X-rays, CT scans, MRI, and ultrasound to aid in accurate medical diagnosis.

Dental

Full range of dental services, from routine check-ups and cleanings to complex restorative and cosmetic dentistry.

Neurosurgery

Surgical treatment for conditions affecting the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves.

Orthopedics

Care for musculoskeletal system disorders, including bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles.

Dermatology & Cosmetology

Diagnosis and treatment of skin, hair, and nail conditions, along with aesthetic and cosmetic procedures.

Anesthesia & Pain Management

Providing safe anesthesia for surgeries and effective management of acute and chronic pain conditions.

Laboratory

Comprehensive laboratory testing services crucial for accurate diagnosis and effective patient management.

Physiotherapy

Rehabilitation services to restore movement, strength, and function for patients recovering from injury or illness.

Psychiatry

Mental health services focusing on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of mental, emotional, and behavioral disorders.

Ophthalmology

Comprehensive eye care services, including diagnosis and treatment of eye diseases, vision correction, and surgical procedures.

Maternity Services

Full range of maternity care, from prenatal to postpartum, ensuring the health and well-being of mother and baby.

Pharmacy & Ambulance

24/7 pharmacy services and emergency ambulance transportation for critical care and patient transfer.

Other Services

Beyond our core specialties, we offer a variety of additional medical and supportive services to ensure holistic patient care.
